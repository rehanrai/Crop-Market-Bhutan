How to Use This Website

1. **To Buy Crops**: Navigate to the "Buy Crops" section, fill out the form with your information and the crops you're interested in, then submit your request.

2. **To Sell Crops**: Go to the "Sell Crops" section, fill out the necessary details regarding the crops you wish to sell, and click the "List Crop" button.

3. **General Instructions**: Make sure to provide accurate information to facilitate the process. Keep an eye on your email for responses to your requests.
